Connect Existing Local DurielBiz POS

Use this if the business already exists locally on a shop computer and you want to start remote monitoring now.

Existing local business supported

Step 1

On the local shop PC, log in as Admin.

Step 2

Open Business Settings in the local POS.

Step 3

Enable Cloud Sync.

Step 4

Paste this page’s Ingest URL and Sync Token.

Step 5

Click Sync Now on the local POS. Existing sales, purchases, inventory logs, and branches will appear here.

Important

  • You do not need to recreate the business on the local POS.
  • The local business stays as it is; you only connect it by adding the ingest URL and sync token.
  • Use the same cloud account for all branches that belong to this business.
